Datamatix will be Celebrating the 25th Anniversary of the largest technology driven event in Dubai, UAE in October 2018, the event that annually brings together regional and global IT leaders, and government and business organizations for developing technlogy management strategies and innovations and technologies that are shaping the future and changing the world.


The event over the past years has attracted most popular global keynotes and experts in its key addresses and interactive sessions, and has presented the most successful best practices that seeks to create new global governance models, leveraging new technologies and modern management strategies whilst focusing on devising strategies specially for the post-oil era and developing AI, blockchain and internet of things for future infrastructure and sovereignty in our increasingly interconnected world.


On the occasion of the 25th anniversary of the launch of the Conference, Datamatix in association with its global partners the, "25th Gitex Conference" with a wide international participation of government and business organizations, leaders decision-makers and expert and international organizations. Continuing the vision of the conference since its launch to support the latest corporate practices and sustainable global excellence strategies, the 25th Conference focuses on developing the relationship between global communities and organizations, where the west-east and north-south purposively meets and sets the stage for strengthening the future development strategy and share global best practices and the organizational innovative strategies. The conference will highlight technology management strategies, innovations and technologies that are shaping the future and changing the world.
